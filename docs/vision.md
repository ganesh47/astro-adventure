# Vision

Astro Adventure is a controller-first astronomy adventure for kids ages 8-12. Players explore space, planets, moons, gravity, orbits, and scientific discovery through missions that feel like play first and lessons second.

## Learning Goals

- Build curiosity about astronomy and space science.
- Teach planetary facts through exploration and interaction.
- Introduce physics concepts such as gravity, mass, motion, thrust, and orbits.
- Reinforce learning with quizzes, badges, and replayable challenges.

## Experience Goals

- Friendly for couch play on Xbox and PC.
- Readable for kids, parents, and classrooms.
- Accessible to controller-first players.
- Built openly, with license-clean assets and research.
