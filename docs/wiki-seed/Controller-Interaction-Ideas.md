# Controller Interaction Ideas

## Goal

Map Xbox controller inputs and vibration to astronomy and physics lessons.

## Ideas

- Triggers control rocket thrust.
- Left stick sets direction or orbit adjustment.
- Right stick controls camera or scanning tools.
- Vibration intensity signals gravity wells or engine force.
- Buttons trigger scans, quizzes, probes, and discovery actions.

## Accessibility Notes

Every vibration-based cue needs a visual or audio alternative.
