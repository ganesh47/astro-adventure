# Astronomy Curriculum

## Goal

Collect age-appropriate astronomy concepts for kids ages 8-12 and shape them into playable missions.

## Candidate Topics

- Solar system scale
- Planets and moons
- Gravity and orbits
- Stars and constellations
- Spacecraft, probes, and rovers
- Observation, evidence, and scientific curiosity

## Design Rule

Each lesson should connect to an action the player performs, not just text they read.
